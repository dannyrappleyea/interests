# Sanscrit Language

## Words
Nivritti: internal world. Opposite of Pravritti. (Source: Yin yoga class)

Pravritti, Pravṛtti: external world, our awareness of it, possibly our actions within it. Opposite of Nivritti. (Source: Yin yoga class)
